Discuss the question below.
Have you ever heard about sea shanties?
(Sea shanties are traditional work songs that were commonly sung by sailors and seafarers during the 19th century and earlier. These songs were an integral part of maritime culture and were used to coordinate tasks and provide rhythm for various activities on ships. Sea shanties were particularly important during tasks that required teamwork and synchronization, such as hauling ropes, raising sails, and turning capstans.)
